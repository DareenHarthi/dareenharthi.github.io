I am a Master's student at Carnegie Mellon University's Language Technologies Institute, working with Professor Bhiksha Raj at the Machine Learning for Signal Processing (MLSP) Lab. Previously, I received my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from IMSIU University, where I worked under the guidance of Professor Areeb Alowisheq. My research focuses on voice biometrics and privacy, specifically exploring methods for biometric estimation from voice signals and developing techniques for voice privacy preservation and manipulation.
