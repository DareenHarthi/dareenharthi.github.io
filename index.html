I am a Master's student at Carnegie Mellon University's Language Technologies Institute, working with Professor Bhiksha Raj at the Machine Learning for Signal Processing (MLSP) Lab. Previously, I received my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Imam Mohammad ibn Saud Islamic University. My research interests include voice privacy, biometric estimation from speech signals, and voice manipulation techniques. I am particularly interested in developing methods to protect speaker privacy while maintaining speech utility.
